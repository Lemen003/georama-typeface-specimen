Georama is an original typeface that is available in several different widths and weights. Georama was created by Production Type, which is a digital type design agency based in Paris, France. I view Georama as being very condensed, yet legible and easy to navigate. Georama makes me think of large cities in that way. Cities can be very condensed with all the buildings and high-rises built on top of one another with massive crowds of people. But cities are still somehow easy to navigate through them. Georama also gives me very professional, clean, business-like vibes with a modern and minimal feel.
